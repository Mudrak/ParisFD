                                        Training

 

The Paris Fire Department conducts continuous education training

 to ensure that all members are ready to assist our community when

the need arises. Lt. Jeff McFarland is the Training Coordinator for

the department.  Training for each shift is carried out by: Lt.

McFarland, Bat Chief. Rick Covington, Firefighter Larry Jones,

and Firefighter Mark Logan.

 

Fire training consists of basic fire operations as well as new

techniques and evolutions. Fire training at the Paris Fire Department

is open to any firefighter in and around Bourbon County, and is

often attended by members of any of the countyÂ’s seven volunteer

departments, as well as by members of the Bourbon County Fire

Department.  Shift training is normally held every week on Tuesdays,

Wednesdays, and Thursdays.

 

The first week of each month, Emergency Medical training takes

place for all shifts.  Current topics in the medical field, as well as

basic patient care curriculum are covered.

 

IF you have any questions for our training staff,  Lt. McFarland can

be reached at fivebugles306@yahoo.com.  A monthly training calendar

will be posted here on the website.
