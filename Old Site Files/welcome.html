Randall R. Stulce, Fire Chief Welcome to Cedar Hammock Fire Rescue On behalf of the Board of Fire Commissioners and our entire staff I would like to welcome you to our web site. To keep you informed as much as we can we continually update our site with information about our department. Let me tell you a little about Cedar Hammock Fire Rescue. Formed as a volunteer department in the late 50's the District provides Fire and Rescue services to the citizens in the Southwest portion of the unincorporated areas of Manatee County, Florida. Now a career department, we hold an ISO rating of 3 and respond to 4,500 emergency calls each year. We protect a seasonal population which ranges between 50,000 to 70,000 in a 22 square mile area. We operate three fire stations and have five Engines and one Truck company. The mission I have set for our department is: "Provide Quality Service with Pride, Commitment, and Courage." If my staff or I can be of any assistance , please contact me.
